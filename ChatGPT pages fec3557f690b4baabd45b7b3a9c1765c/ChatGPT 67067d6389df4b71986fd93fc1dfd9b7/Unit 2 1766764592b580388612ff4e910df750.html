Q6. What are the various catalysts suitable for Overfitting? Explain.

  1. High Model Complexity:
    • Too many features or deep trees cause overfitting.
  1. Insufficient Training Data:
    • Model memorizes limited data instead of generalizing.
  1. Too Many Parameters:
    • Large neural networks overfit small datasets.
  1. No Regularization:
    • L1/L2 regularization helps prevent overfitting.
  1. Small Train-Test Split:
    • Using 90% train & 10% test may cause overfitting.

Q7. What are the various estimation techniques for multi-class classification algorithms? Explain with an example.

  1. One-vs-All (OvA)
    • Each class is treated as a binary problem.
    • Example: Classifying digits (0-9) separately as (0 vs. rest), (1 vs. rest), etc.
  1. One-vs-One (OvO)
    • Each pair of classes is compared separately.
    • Example: Comparing A vs B, A vs C, B vs C, etc.
  1. Softmax Regression
    • Assigns probabilities to each class.
    • Example: Predicting spam vs. non-spam emails.
  1. Decision Tree / Random Forest
    • Assigns class labels by majority vote.
